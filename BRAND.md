## Naming criteria

When choosing the final name, the bar is:

1. **`.dev` or `.com` domain available** at registrar pricing (not aftermarket).
2. **npm scope available** as `@<name>/*`.
3. **GitHub org name available** for the public repo.
4. **Pronounceable in 4 syllables or fewer.** People type it; people say it on calls.
5. **Doesn't presuppose Atlassian.** This is an MCP-wide product.
6. **Doesn't presuppose "AI" or "LLM"** in the name. MCP is the durable concept; "AI" and "LLM" date fast.
7. **No active trademark conflict** in software/security category (USPTO + EUIPO quick check).
8. **Not a single word easily Googled into a different established product.**
